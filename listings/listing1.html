Beach Villa By The Sea

About this property

Beachfront Location: Rooms at Castle from XVI century Zamagna in Zaton offers direct beachfront access with stunning sea views. Guests can relax on the sun terrace or explore the lush garden.

Comfortable Accommodations: Each room features a private bathroom, air-conditioning, and a work desk. Additional amenities include free WiFi throughout the property, a patio, and a wardrobe.

Convenient Facilities: The guest house provides free on-site private parking, an outdoor fireplace, and a shared kitchen. Outdoor seating and picnic areas enhance the stay.

Nearby Attractions: Soline Beach is a 15-minute walk away, while Štikovica Beach lies 1.2 mi from the property. Dubrovnik Airport is 17 mi distant. Perfect for a peaceful weekend getaway. Includes a fireplace, private deck, and modern kitchen.
