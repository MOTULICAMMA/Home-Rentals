Jankó Apartman

About this property

About: Jankó Apartman offers accommodations with private terraces in the center of Siófok. With pool views, this accommodation provides a balcony and a swimming pool. The guest house features rooms with air conditioning, free private parking, and free Wifi.

Comfortable Accommodations: At the guest house, units include a wardrobe, a TV, a private bathroom, bed linen, and towels. A microwave, a toaster, and fridge are also offered, as well as a coffee machine and a kettle. The rooms are equipped with heating facilities.

Convenient Facilities: The area is popular for cycling and hiking, and bike rental is available at the guest house. The guest house also comes with outdoor fireplace and a picnic area for a day outdoors.

Nearby Attractions: Ujhelyi Beach is 1.4 miles from Jankó Apartman, while Bella Stables and Animal Park is 5.9 miles from the property.
